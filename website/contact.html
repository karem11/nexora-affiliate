Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell products directly?

No. NEXORA is a discovery platform — we curate trending products available on Amazon. All purchases are completed on Amazon.com under Amazon's own checkout, shipping, and return process.

How do you choose which products to feature?

We use an automated analysis tool that combines Amazon Best Sellers data, Google Trends, Pinterest, TikTok signals, and verified review counts. The top performers across multiple categories make it onto our site. Read more on our About page.

Can I suggest a product?

Absolutely. Use the form above with the subject "Product Recommendation" and tell us about it. If it fits our quality bar, we'll consider adding it.

I bought a product through your link and have a problem with it. Can you help?

Unfortunately, we cannot directly help with Amazon orders. Once you complete a purchase, the transaction is between you and Amazon. Please contact Amazon Customer Service at amazon.com/contact. We are happy to hear feedback about the product so we can update our listings.

Are you available for partnerships or business inquiries?

Yes. Use the form above with the subject "Partnership / Business" and we'll get back to you.
