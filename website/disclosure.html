Affiliate Disclosure

Full transparency about how NEXORA operates and earns revenue

Last updated: November 2026

📢 Quick summary: NEXORA participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click a product link on our site and make a qualifying purchase on Amazon, we earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. The price you pay is exactly the same as if you visited Amazon directly.

1. What Is an Affiliate Disclosure?

This page exists to be fully transparent with you about how NEXORA operates and how we make money. The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) requires websites that use affiliate links to clearly disclose their affiliate relationships. We take this seriously because trust is everything.

2. Amazon Associates Program

NEXORA is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

This means that when you click on a product link on our Site and complete a qualifying purchase on Amazon, NEXORA may receive a small commission from Amazon. Amazon pays us; you do not pay any extra.

3. How Affiliate Links Work

Every product on NEXORA links to Amazon.com using a special tracking link that contains our Amazon Associates ID (Store ID: kareemelsay0a-20). When you click an affiliate link:

  1. You are redirected to the product page on Amazon.com.
  2. Amazon sets a cookie that lasts for 24 hours (or 90 days if you add the item to your cart).
  3. If you complete a purchase within that window — even if it is a different product than the one you originally clicked — we may earn a commission.
  4. The price you pay is the standard Amazon price. There is no markup, no surcharge, no hidden fee.

4. Why We Use Affiliate Links

Affiliate commissions are how NEXORA stays free for you. Without them, we could not afford to:

  • Research and curate trending products every day;
  • Maintain the Site, hosting, and software;
  • Keep our content free of intrusive pop-up ads, paywalls, or sponsored "spam";
  • Continuously improve our recommendation algorithm.

We believe affiliate revenue is the most ethical way to fund a recommendation site, because we only earn money when you actually find something useful.

5. Our Editorial Promise

The fact that we earn affiliate commissions does not influence which products we feature on NEXORA. Our recommendations are based on:

  • Topical relevance and category match;
  • Active community engagement signals;
  • Trend signals from multiple sources (Google Trends, Amazon Best Sellers, Pinterest, TikTok);
  • Our own analysis of value for money.

We do not accept payment from brands to feature their products. We do not change rankings based on commission rates. If we recommend a product, it is because we genuinely believe it offers good value.

6. Required Statement (Amazon Associates)

Per the requirements of the Amazon Associates Operating Agreement, we are required to display the following statement:

"NEXORA is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com."

You will see a shorter version of this statement in the footer of every page on our Site.

7. Product Information

NEXORA does not display prices, star ratings, or review counts on this site. All current pricing, availability, ratings, and reviews are shown on Amazon.com. Please review those details on the product page on Amazon before purchasing.

We feature products based on our own editorial selection, trend signals, and community engagement. Amazon is the authoritative source for all pricing and inventory information.

8. Other Affiliate Programs

Currently, NEXORA only participates in the Amazon Associates Program. If we add other affiliate programs in the future (e.g., other retailers), we will update this disclosure to list them clearly.
