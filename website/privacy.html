Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: November 2026  ·  Effective date: November 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to NEXORA ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate the website nexora-shop-us.netlify.app (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our Site.

By using NEXORA, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

NEXORA is a static product discovery site. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or submit personal information to browse our content. However, like most websites, we automatically collect certain non-personal information through your browser and through third-party services.

2.1 Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent on each page, referring website, approximate geographic location (country/region only), and device/browser type.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: we use cookies to remember preferences and improve user experience.
  • Log data: our hosting provider (Netlify) automatically logs IP addresses for security purposes.

2.2 Information you may provide voluntarily:

  • If you contact us via email, we receive the email address and any content you choose to send.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our Site;
  • Understand which products and categories users are interested in;
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activity;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Affiliate Links and Amazon Associates

NEXORA is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

When you click an affiliate link on our Site and make a qualifying purchase on Amazon, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. The commission helps us maintain and grow NEXORA. For full details, please read our Affiliate Disclosure.

Amazon may set its own cookies when you click an affiliate link. Amazon's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Notice, available at amazon.com/privacy.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

NEXORA uses cookies and browser localStorage in a minimal, privacy-respecting way. For full details please read our Cookie Policy.

Specifically:

  • Cookie consent (localStorage): when you click "Accept" on the cookie banner, we save a small flag (nexora_cookie_consent) in your browser so we don't show the banner again. This flag stays on your device only — we never receive it.
  • Strictly necessary cookies: may be set by our hosting provider (Netlify) for basic site security and performance.
  • Analytics (future): if we add a privacy-respecting analytics tool such as Plausible or GA4, we will update this Privacy Policy and the Cookie Policy first.
  • Affiliate tracking cookies (Amazon): when you click a product link to Amazon, Amazon sets its own cookies on amazon.com. Those cookies are governed by Amazon's privacy policy, not NEXORA's.

You can disable cookies and clear local storage at any time from your browser settings. Disabling them may affect parts of the Site.

5b. AI-Generated Content

Some product titles, summaries, hooks, benefits, and lifestyle images on NEXORA are generated or enhanced using artificial intelligence (e.g., Google Gemini for text, Pollinations / similar models for images). AI-generated content is reviewed and curated by our team, but may contain inaccuracies. Amazon's product page is always the authoritative source for product specifications, pricing, ratings, and availability.

We do not feed any of your personal data into AI services. The AI models we use only see public product metadata (e.g., the product name and category) when generating marketing copy.

6. Third-Party Services

NEXORA uses the following third-party services:

  • Netlify — hosting and content delivery (netlify.com/privacy);
  • Amazon Associates — affiliate program for product links (amazon.com/privacy);
  • Google Fonts — for typography (policies.google.com/privacy).

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information by using HTTPS (TLS) encryption for all connections to our Site. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

NEXORA is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

  • The right to access the personal information we have about you;
  • The right to correct any inaccurate information;
  • The right to delete your personal information;
  • The right to object to or restrict processing of your information;
  • The right to data portability;

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

10. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. NEXORA does not currently respond to DNT signals because there is no industry-standard implementation. We will update this policy if we adopt a standard for handling DNT.
